Best movies according to Nethmini

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Me Before You

It is a romantic drama film based on the book Me Before You by Jojo Moyes.
Full of laughter and tears at the end

Sunny

South Korean comedy-drama film is about a middle-aged woman who tries to fulfil
her friend's dying wish of reuniting their group of high school friends.
Humour, happiness and tears, platonic love.

Five Feet Apart

It's a film based on the book Five Feet Apart written by Rachael Lippincott.
A pair of teenagers with cystic fibrosis who meet in a hospital and fall in love, though their disease means they must avoid close physical contact.
It has a hidden message of how precious a human life is.
